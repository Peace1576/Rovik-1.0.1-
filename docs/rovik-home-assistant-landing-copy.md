- Eve combines inbox, calendar, bills, tasks, reminders, and device actions into one operational layer. She does not just answer questions. She finds what needs attention, prepares actions, asks for approval when needed, and helps your household stay ahead.

- Compare home purchases, track needs, and find practical ways to save money.
